Conditional independence

Is photosynthesis independent of treatment given water content?

  • Conditional multual information. \(I(X,Y|Z)=E(\log\frac{P(X,Y|Z)}{P(X|Z)P(Y|Z)})\). It is proportional to the log-likelihood ratio test \(G^2\) (they differ by a 2n factor, where n is the sample size) and it is related to the deviance of the tested models(Scutari, M. (2009), page 5, https://arxiv.org/pdf/0908.3817.pdf, ci.test).

    Mutual Information (cond. Gauss.)
